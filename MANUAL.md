## Introduction

### What is AFHhub?

AFHhub is a comprehensive, unified platform designed specifically for the Adult Family Home (AFH) industry in Washington State. It serves as a central portal that brings together three powerful applications, each addressing critical needs in the AFH ecosystem:

- **AFH Provider Search**: Connect with 580+ verified Adult Family Home providers
- **AFH Property Advisor**: Analyze and plan property conversions for AFH operations
- **AFH Resident Placement Services**: Intelligently match residents with appropriate providers

### Who Should Use AFHhub?

AFHhub is designed for multiple user types:

**For Property Owners:**
- Individuals looking to lease properties to AFH providers
- Real estate investors interested in AFH conversions
- Property managers handling AFH-related properties
- Homeowners considering converting their property for AFH use

**For Healthcare Professionals:**
- Case managers placing residents in AFH facilities
- Hospital discharge coordinators
- Rehabilitation facility managers
- Social workers assisting with senior care placement

**For AFH Providers:**

**For Potential AFH Operators:**

- Entrepreneurs exploring the AFH market

## AFH Provider Search Guide

### Accessing Provider Search

**Method 1: From Landing Page**
1. Scroll to the "AFH Provider Search" section (blue gradient)
2. Click the "Access AFH Provider Search" button
3. You'll be directed to the provider search application

**Method 2: Direct Navigation**
1. Click "Provider Search" in the navigation menu
2. Or use the quick link in the footer

### Basic Search Operations

**Simple Text Search:**
1. Enter a search term in the search bar
2. Results update in real-time as you type
3. Try searching for:
   - City names: "Centralia", "Seattle", "Tacoma"
   - Payment types: "Medicaid", "Private Pay"
   - Business types: "LLC", "AFH", "Home"
   - Specialties: "Dementia", "Developmental Disabilities"

**Quick Filter Buttons:**
- Click pre-configured filter buttons for instant results
- Common filters: "Centralia", "Medicaid", "LLC"
- Search bar auto-populates when clicking filters
- Results update immediately

**Phone Number Search:**
- Enter full or partial phone numbers
- Example: "360" finds all 360 area code providers
- Example: "360807" finds specific number ranges
- Perfect when you have a number but not the business name

### Advanced Search Techniques

**Combining Multiple Terms:**
- Type multiple words separated by spaces
- Example: "Centralia Medicaid" finds Medicaid providers in Centralia
- Example: "LLC 360" finds LLC providers with 360 area code
- Platform matches ALL terms - order doesn't matter

**Search Strategies:**
1. **Location-Based**: Start with city name, then add criteria
2. **Payment-Focused**: Search payment type, then location
3. **Specialty-Driven**: Search specialty, then filter by location
4. **Business Type**: Search structure type, then add location
5. **Combination**: Use multiple criteria for precise results

### Understanding Search Results

**Provider Table Columns:**
- **Area/City**: Location information (e.g., "Centralia, WA")
- **Business Name**: Official registered business name
- **Provider Name**: Contact person name
- **Phone Number**: Direct contact number (click-to-call on mobile)
- **Website**: External links to provider websites
- **Year Started**: Years of business experience
- **Resident Beds**: Current bed capacity
- **Payment Ratio**: Private Pay vs Medicaid percentages
- **Others**: Additional information and specialties

**Sorting Results:**
- Click column headers to sort
- Click again to reverse sort order
- Visual indicators (â–²/â–¼) show sort direction
- Sort by: Area, Business Name, Year Started, Resident Beds

**Interpreting Provider Information:**
- **Experience**: Higher years = more established
- **Capacity**: Bed count indicates facility size
- **Payment Types**: Check what payment methods accepted
- **Specialties**: Review "Others" column for detailed services

### Contacting Providers

**Mobile Devices:**
- Tap phone numbers to open dialer automatically
- Click website links to visit provider sites
- Use contact information for direct communication

**Desktop:**
- Right-click phone numbers to copy
- Click website links to open in new tabs
- Use contact forms if available on provider websites

**Best Practices:**
- Call during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- Have questions prepared before calling
- Verify current availability and payment acceptance
- Follow up if needed
- Document contact attempts and responses

### Provider Comparison

**Side-by-Side Comparison:**
1. Search for matching criteria
2. Sort by important metric (experience, capacity, etc.)
3. Review multiple providers in sorted list
4. Document key differences
5. Compare: Experience, capacity, payment types, specialties, location

## AFH Resident Placement Services Guide

### Accessing Placement Services

**Method 1: From Landing Page**
1. Scroll to the "AFH Resident Placement Services" section (indigo gradient)
2. Click the "Access AFH Resident Placement Services" button
3. You'll be directed to the placement services application

**Method 2: Direct Navigation**
1. Click "Placement Services" in the navigation menu
2. Or use the quick link in the footer

### Adding a New Resident

**Resident Intake Form:**
1. Navigate to "Add New Resident" page
2. Fill in demographic information:
   - Name, date of birth, age (auto-calculated)
   - Current location
   - Discharge date
   - Priority level (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
3. Enter care requirements:
   - Required care level
   - Payment type (Medicaid, Private Pay, Both)
   - Medical conditions
   - Special needs
4. Add case manager information
5. Submit form to create resident record

**Required Information:**
- Full name
- Date of birth
- Current location
- Care level required
- Payment type
- Discharge date (if applicable)
- Priority level

**Optional Information:**
- Medical conditions
- Special needs
- Preferences
- Family contact information
- Insurance information

### Generating Matches

**Automatic Matching Process:**
1. Select a resident from the list
2. Click "Generate Matches" button
3. System analyzes all providers
4. Calculates match scores (0-100%)
5. Displays top 10 matches sorted by score

**Understanding Match Scores:**
- **90-100%**: Excellent match, highly recommended
- **80-89%**: Very good match, strong recommendation
- **70-79%**: Good match, viable option
- **60-69%**: Acceptable match, consider alternatives
- **Below 60%**: Poor match, not recommended

**Match Reasoning:**
Each match includes detailed reasoning:
- Care level compatibility explanation
- Payment type match status
- Availability information
- Location proximity details
- Specialty match analysis
- Response time factors

### Reviewing and Accepting Matches

**Match Review Process:**
1. Review match scores and reasoning
2. Click on individual matches for details
3. Compare multiple matches side-by-side
4. Check provider availability
5. Review provider profiles
6. Contact providers if needed
7. Accept matches to proceed

**Accepting a Match:**
1. Click "Accept Match" button
2. Confirm acceptance
3. Match status updates to "Accepted"
4. Provider receives notification (if configured)
5. Proceed with placement process

**Rejecting a Match:**
1. Click "Reject Match" button
2. Optionally provide rejection reason
3. Match status updates to "Rejected"
4. System may suggest alternatives

### Managing Residents

**Resident Dashboard:**
- View all residents in one place
- Filter by status, priority, case manager
- Sort by various criteria
- Quick actions for common tasks
- Status indicators

**Resident Status Types:**
- **Pending**: Newly added, no matches yet
- **Matching**: Matches being generated
- **Matched**: Matches available for review
- **Accepted**: Match accepted, placement in progress
- **Placed**: Successfully placed with provider
- **Closed**: Case closed (placed elsewhere or cancelled)

**Priority Levels:**
- **Urgent**: Immediate placement needed (within 24-48 hours)
- **High**: Placement needed soon (within 1 week)
- **Medium**: Standard placement timeline (1-2 weeks)
- **Low**: Flexible timeline (2+ weeks)

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is AFHhub free to use?**
A: Yes, AFHhub is completely free. No registration, no fees, no credit card required.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No account creation is required. The platform is accessible immediately.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: All modern browsers are supported (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we follow industry best practices for data security and privacy.

**Q: How often is data updated?**
A: Provider data is updated regularly from official sources. Updates occur as new information becomes available.

### Provider Search Questions

**Q: How many providers are in the database?**
A: Over 580 verified providers from Washington State.

**Q: Can I search by phone number?**
A: Yes, you can search by full or partial phone numbers.

**Q: Are all providers verified?**
A: Yes, all providers come from the Adult Family Home Council of Washington State.

**Q: How do I contact a provider?**
A: Click phone numbers on mobile for direct calling, or visit provider websites for contact forms.

**Q: Can I save favorite providers?**
A: This feature is planned for future updates. Currently, you can bookmark providers in your browser.

### Property Advisor Questions

**Q: What is WABO?**
A: WABO stands for Washington Association of Building Officials. WABO compliance is required for AFH conversions.

**Q: How accurate are cost estimates?**
A: Estimates are based on typical conversion costs. Actual costs may vary based on specific property conditions.

**Q: Can I use the marketing materials directly?**
A: Yes, marketing templates are ready to use. Customize with your specific property details.

**Q: How long does conversion take?**
A: Timeline varies by scenario: 30-90 days (direct lease), 90-120 days (minimal conversion), 120-180 days (full conversion).

**Q: What if my property doesn't fit the scenarios?**
A: Contact a professional for custom analysis. Scenarios are general guidelines.

### Placement Services Questions

**Q: How does the matching algorithm work?**
A: The algorithm uses weighted scoring across multiple criteria: care level (30%), payment type (25%), availability (15%), location (15%), specialties (10%), and response time (5%).

**Q: How accurate are the matches?**
A: Match accuracy is 85%+ based on weighted scoring. Always verify matches with providers directly.

**Q: Can I prioritize urgent cases?**
A: Yes, set priority level to "Urgent" when adding residents. Urgent cases are automatically prioritized.

**Q: How many matches will I get?**
A: The system provides up to 10 top matches sorted by score.

**Q: What if I don't like any matches?**
A: You can reject matches and generate new ones, or adjust resident criteria and search again.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why is the page loading slowly?**
A: Check your internet connection. Clear browser cache. Close other browser tabs. Try a different browser.

**Q: Why aren't my search results showing?**
A: Make sure JavaScript is enabled. Try refreshing the page. Clear your search and try different terms.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**
A: Yes, AFHhub is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No installation required. AFHhub runs entirely in your web browser.

**Q: Is there an app version?**
A: Currently, AFHhub is web-based. Mobile app versions are planned for future updates.
